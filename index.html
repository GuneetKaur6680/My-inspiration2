My Favourite Hobby is
Horse Riding

Hi!

Horse riding is my favourite hobby. Horse riding as a hobby brings the me closer to nature. When I ride through the fields and farms. I can see the local terrain from a special vantage point. I have the wide open spaces all around them. A beautiful blue sky through a sun roof of a car is just not the same as a completely open view from the back of a horse. The cool fresh air away from the city is usually clear and clean. Horse riding as a hobby is very relaxing and fun for me. The pressures of every day life seem to disappear on the back of a horse.
